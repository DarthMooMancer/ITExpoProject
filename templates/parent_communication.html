Principal Rodriguez
October 10, 2025
School Safety Update

Dear Parents and Guardians,

I hope this message finds you well. I wanted to inform you about some important safety updates for our school.

We have recently implemented a new visitor management system. All visitors must now present a valid ID and will receive a temporary badge while on campus. This helps us ensure that only authorized individuals have access to our school buildings.

Additionally, we have added new security cameras around the perimeter of the school property to enhance our monitoring capabilities.

If you have any questions or concerns about these changes, please don't hesitate to contact the main office.

Thank you for your continued support,
Principal Rodriguez
