Why This Page Exists
Today

This page was created as a demonstration of what NOT to do when designing a website. The colors are intentionally clashing, the animations are distracting, and the overall design is meant to be as unpleasant as possible.

In web design, it's important to maintain a consistent color scheme, use readable fonts, and create a user-friendly experience. This page does the opposite of all those principles.

Color Theory Gone Wrong
Yesterday

In proper color theory, we use complementary colors, maintain proper contrast, and ensure text is readable. This page uses:

  • Bright magenta background
  • Cyan text
  • Bright yellow accents
  • Bright green and red highlights
  • Bright blue shadows

All of these colors together create a visual nightmare that's hard to look at for more than a few seconds.

Typography Mistakes
Last Week

This page uses Comic Sans MS, which is widely considered one of the most unprofessional fonts. The text sizes are inconsistent, and there's no clear hierarchy.

Proper typography should be:

  • Readable
  • Consistent
  • Professional
  • Hierarchical

This page fails on all counts.
